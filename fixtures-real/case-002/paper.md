# Review Patch Planner

## Title

Review Patch Planner for Paper Revision Loops

## Abstract

We propose a planner that converts peer-review comments into patch-sized revision tasks. The planner keeps unsupported requests out of the author queue and bundles each accepted task with a target claim, evidence source, and verification command.

## Claims

1. The planner converts 34 of 40 reviewer comments into patch-sized revision tasks.
2. The unsupported-request filter rejects 9 of 10 off-scope review comments.
3. The paper suggests the planner may reduce author revision time, but no timed author study has been run.

## Evidence

The revision table reports 34 patch-sized tasks from 40 reviewer comments.
The filter audit reports 9 rejected off-scope review comments out of 10 unsupported requests.
The discussion says no timed author study has been run.

## Limitations

The current evaluation uses synthetic reviewer comments and saved paper drafts. Human timing evidence is absent.
