# Citation Trace Verifier

## Claims

1. The verifier preserves 31 of 32 citation links after trace compression.
2. The paper includes a retrieval-only baseline in Appendix A.
3. It has not been evaluated on multilingual papers.

## Evidence

The citation audit reports 31 preserved links out of 32 after compression. Appendix A lists the retrieval-only baseline. The limitation section says multilingual papers are not evaluated.
