# Experiment Card Generator

## Claims

1. The generator emits bounded keep and discard conditions for 14 of 15 review criticisms.
2. The output schema is validated by a deterministic JSON checker.
3. It does not test whether researchers can execute the generated cards within one day.

## Evidence

The audit table shows bounded keep and discard conditions in 14 of 15 cards. The implementation section describes a deterministic JSON checker. The paper says one-day researcher execution has not been tested.
