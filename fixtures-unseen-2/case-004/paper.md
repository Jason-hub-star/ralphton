# Tool Failure Taxonomy

## Claims

1. The taxonomy labels timeout, permission, and parse-error failures in 40 agent runs.
2. The paper reports a manual spot-check of 10 labels.
3. The taxonomy is not evaluated on browser automation tasks.

## Evidence

The dataset has 40 agent runs and labels timeout, permission, and parse-error failures. The validation section reports a manual spot-check of 10 labels. The limitation section says browser automation tasks are not included.
