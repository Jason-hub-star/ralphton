# Review-to-Revision Planner

## Claims

1. The planner converts 11 of 13 actionable review comments into revision tasks.
2. It preserves all explicit reviewer constraints in the generated task list.
3. No comparison against a human-written revision plan is included.

## Evidence

The conversion audit reports 11 actionable comments converted into revision tasks. The constraint audit says all explicit reviewer constraints are preserved. The comparison section says no human-written revision plan baseline is included.
