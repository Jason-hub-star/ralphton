# Revision Patch Planner

## Claims

1. The planner creates patch-sized tasks for 16 of 18 reviewer requests.
2. The paper reports a human-written revision plan baseline.
3. It does not test cross-paper transfer.

## Evidence

The task table lists patch-sized tasks for 16 of 18 reviewer requests. The baseline section reports a human-written revision plan baseline. The limitation section says cross-paper transfer is not tested.
