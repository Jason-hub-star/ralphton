# Agent Workflow Benchmark

## Claims

1. The loop scheduler reduces idle tool time from 42 minutes to 18 minutes on 12 Auto Research tasks.
2. The benchmark reports the planner-only baseline and the scheduler result in Table 1.
3. The study leaves model-tier cost ablations for future work.

## Evidence

Table 1 lists planner-only idle time at 42 minutes and scheduled idle time at 18 minutes. The benchmark covers 12 Auto Research tasks. The paper says model-tier cost ablations are future work.
