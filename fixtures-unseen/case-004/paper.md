# Review Router Ablation

## Claims

1. The router filters rubric-irrelevant comments in 8 of 10 synthetic reviews.
2. The paper reports a calibration curve over confidence buckets.
3. The evaluation has not been run with adversarial reviewer personas.

## Evidence

The ablation table lists 8 filtered rubric-irrelevant comments out of 10 synthetic reviews. Figure 2 is a calibration curve over low, medium, and high confidence buckets. The limitation section says adversarial reviewer personas have not been run.
