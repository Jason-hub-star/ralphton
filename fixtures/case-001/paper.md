# Korean Source Fallback

## Claims

1. The baseline source enrichment success rate is 27% on the saved 30-item fixture.
2. Korean-language items form the largest identifiable failure cluster.
3. A Korean-language fallback should improve enrichment success rate to at least 50%.

## Evidence

The baseline run enriched 8 out of 30 items. Among the 22 failures, 8 were Korean-language items.

No post-fallback run has been executed yet.
