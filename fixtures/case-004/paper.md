# Auto Review Calibration

## Claims

1. The review calibrator catches 6 unsupported criticisms out of 20 synthetic reviews.
2. The calibrator preserves all grounded criticisms in the same fixture.
3. The calibrator does not evaluate reviewer tone or writing style.

## Evidence

The fixture report marks 6 unsupported criticisms and 14 grounded criticisms. The output preserves all 14 grounded criticisms. Tone and writing style are excluded from scope.
