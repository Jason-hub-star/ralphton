# Experiment Card Selector

## Claims

1. Selecting the cheapest decisive experiment reduces wasted loop iterations.
2. In the saved 12-review fixture, the selector avoids 4 off-scope reviewer requests.
3. The selector has not been evaluated with live LLM-generated reviews.

## Evidence

The 12-review fixture includes 4 off-scope reviewer requests. The selector routes all 4 to ignore or defer. No live LLM-generated review test has been run.
